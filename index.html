What is FreshGuard?

Biodegradable sticker. Instant clarity.

FreshGuard is a biodegradable freshness indicator sticker designed to help consumers and retailers instantly determine whether food is still safe to consume.

By responding to natural changes in food chemistry, the sticker shifts color as spoilage begins, giving users a clear and reliable signal at a glance.

How it works

Simple 3-step freshness flow

This visual cue replaces the guesswork of expiry dates.

1

Apply

FreshGuard is placed directly on food packaging. The sticker remains clear and neutral while food is fresh.

2

Detect

As food begins to spoil it releases compounds that alter the local pH. FreshGuard detects these changes instantly.

3

Reveal

Natural dyes transition through colors to signal Fresh (safe), Caution (consume soon), or Spoiled (not safe).

The science behind FreshGuard

Natural chemistry inside every sticker

FreshGuard uses a combination of natural, food-safe dyes embedded in a transparent biopolymer.

Transparent biopolymer film

Biodegradable and environmentally friendly layer.

Natural bio-indicator dyes

Anthocyanin (red cabbage, berries) and Curcumin (turmeric) shift color based on pH and spoilage gases.

Food-safe adhesive

Strong bond, safe for direct packaging contact.

Smart material stack

Sensitive, accurate reactions to chemical signals without electronics.
